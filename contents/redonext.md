A complete engagement engine designed for FMCG, oil companies, automotive parts, beverage brands & nationwide campaigns.

### Key Features:

- QR Scan Engine (Unique Code / Product Tracking)
- OTP Verification & Device Tracking
- Spin Wheel + Flip Card Game System
- Probability-Based Reward Logic
- Digital Wallet & Points Management
- Reward Redemption (Gifts / Cash / Recharge)
- Retailer / Mechanic / Driver Profiles
- Geo-tagged Scans for Distribution Analytics
- Fraud Detection & Abuse Prevention
- Powerful Admin Dashboard with Visualization

### Business Impact:

- Increases customer engagement 10x
- Boosts brand loyalty
- Tracks nationwide sales distribution
- Helps identify high-performing dealers
- Reduces fraudulent claims
