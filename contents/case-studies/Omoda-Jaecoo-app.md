# Omoda & Jaecoo Bangladesh App - Current Features

## 📱 App Overview

The Omoda & Jaecoo Bangladesh mobile application is a comprehensive automotive platform built with React Native and Expo, featuring full e-commerce capabilities, service management, finance tools, and vehicle information systems for two premium Chinese automotive brands newly launched in Bangladesh.

**Version**: 1.0.0  
**Package**: com.omodajaecoo.bd.app  
**Platform**: Android & iOS  
**Framework**: React Native with Expo SDK 54.0.0  
**Backend**: Supabase (PostgreSQL)  
**Last Updated**: May 11, 2026
